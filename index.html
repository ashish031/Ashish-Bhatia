Wanderers Stop

The database is MongoDb which is a NoSQL database. Here, a user can post and see various campgrounds. He can also share his reviews on other 's posts(campgrounds). Dice Game

Two players play at single time against each other. The goal is to get 20 or more points. One who gets required points firstly, will be the winner. Each player can roll the dice. Their total current score gets add up. But, if one gets a roll of dice as 1, he will loose all his current points and the turn get shifted to the other player. Players can also hold their score, then turn get shifted to other player and score is stored. This project is made by using HTML, CSS and Javascript.
